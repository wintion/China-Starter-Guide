## Quick Answer
Beijing is excellent for history and museums. Stay central for easy metro access and plan for ticketed sites.

## Why this matters
Major sights can require reservations and long travel times. A smart base reduces daily travel stress.

## Step-by-step
1. Choose a central area such as Dongcheng, Xicheng, or near major metro lines.
2. Plan for major sights on separate days to avoid overload.
3. Use the metro for most trips and taxis for late night returns.
4. Reserve time sensitive attractions early and verify locally for entry rules.
5. Keep pacing slower if you plan a Great Wall day trip.

## Common problems & fixes
- Overpacked days: focus on one major sight per day.
- Long travel times: use taxis for point to point trips.
- Ticket requirements unclear: confirm booking steps before arrival.

## Checklist
- [ ] Central area chosen for lodging.
- [ ] Metro lines noted for daily routes.
- [ ] Reservations confirmed.
- [ ] Day trip pacing planned.
