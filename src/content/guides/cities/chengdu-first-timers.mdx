## Quick Answer
Chengdu is slower paced and easy to explore. Stay central for food and short trips to nearby parks or sites.

## Why this matters
The city is spread out but not difficult to navigate. A central base keeps food and sights close.

## Step-by-step
1. Pick central areas near major metro lines for easy access.
2. Plan one early morning for popular attractions to avoid crowds.
3. Use the metro for most trips and taxis for late evenings.
4. Keep an open day for tea houses or parks to enjoy the pace.
5. Verify locally for ticket or reservation requirements.

## Common problems & fixes
- Long lines at popular spots: arrive early or go on weekdays.
- Spicy food surprises: ask for mild options when ordering.
- Slow travel between districts: use taxis to save time.

## Checklist
- [ ] Central base chosen.
- [ ] Early visit planned for popular spots.
- [ ] Food preferences noted.
- [ ] Backup transport ready.
