## Quick Answer
These cities work well as a paired trip. Use fast rail between them and stay near central business or transit areas.

## Why this matters
Both cities are large and modern. Clear neighborhood choices reduce daily travel time.

## Step-by-step
1. Decide whether you want more time in Guangzhou for food and history or Shenzhen for modern districts.
2. Use high speed rail for the transfer and keep the travel day light.
3. Choose central areas near major metro interchanges for each city.
4. Plan one main activity per day and allow time for rest.
5. Verify locally for any special events or closures.

## Common problems & fixes
- Long travel days: limit city changes to once during the trip.
- Confusing metro systems: use map apps and stick to direct lines.
- Late night returns: take a taxi or ride hailing.

## Checklist
- [ ] Time split between the two cities decided.
- [ ] Transfer day planned with buffer.
- [ ] Central areas selected.
- [ ] Transport apps ready.
