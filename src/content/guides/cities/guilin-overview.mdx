## Quick Answer
Guilin works best with one base and a day trip to nearby scenery areas. Keep transfers short.

## Why this matters
The region is scenic but spread out. A focused plan reduces travel fatigue.

## Step-by-step
1. Choose a base near the riverfront or a central transport hub.
2. Plan one full day for the main landscape area and one day for city sights.
3. Use local transport or guided day trips to avoid navigation issues.
4. Keep evenings light to recover from outdoor days.
5. Verify locally for ticket and weather updates.

## Common problems & fixes
- Too many day trips: choose one highlight and skip the rest.
- Weather changes: move indoor visits to rainy days.
- Long transport to sites: book a shared shuttle or hire a driver.

## Checklist
- [ ] Base location selected.
- [ ] One main landscape day planned.
- [ ] Transport options confirmed.
- [ ] Weather backup ready.
