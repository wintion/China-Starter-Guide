## Quick Answer
Plan enough time for at least one city plus a second stop. Add extra days if you want day trips or slower pacing.

## Why this matters
China is large and travel time adds up. A realistic trip length keeps your itinerary enjoyable.

## Step-by-step
1. List your top priorities and match them to one or two cities.
2. Add a rest day after long flights or transfers.
3. Decide if you want nature or a third city, then add the time needed.
4. Keep one flexible day in case of delays or weather.
5. Verify locally for holiday travel periods that can affect schedules.

## Common problems & fixes
- Trip feels too short: reduce the number of cities.
- Too much time in transit: choose closer city pairs.
- Limited energy: schedule slower mornings or fewer attractions.

## Checklist
- [ ] Priorities matched to cities.
- [ ] Rest day added.
- [ ] Flexible day planned.
- [ ] Travel times confirmed.
