## Quick Answer
Shanghai is easy to navigate and great for modern city views. Stay near central metro lines for quick access.

## Why this matters
The city is spread out. A central base keeps travel time low and makes day trips easier.

## Step-by-step
1. Choose areas like Huangpu, Jing an, or near central metro interchanges.
2. Plan walking days for riverfront and historic neighborhoods.
3. Use the metro for most trips and taxis for late night rides.
4. Keep one day for a nearby water town or museum visit.
5. Verify locally for attraction booking rules.

## Common problems & fixes
- Long distances between sights: split them across multiple days.
- Evening transit crowds: take a taxi for the return.
- Confusing station exits: use a map app and exit numbers.

## Checklist
- [ ] Central area selected.
- [ ] Walking and metro days planned.
- [ ] Day trip option identified.
- [ ] Backup transport ready.
