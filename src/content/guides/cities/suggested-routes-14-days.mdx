## Quick Answer
Two major cities plus one regional stop is a strong 14 day plan. Keep transfers short and allow rest days.

## Why this matters
Two weeks gives you flexibility, but too many stops can still feel rushed.

## Step-by-step
1. Choose two major cities for history and modern experiences.
2. Add one regional city or nature area for contrast.
3. Allocate at least a few nights in each city to avoid constant packing.
4. Use high speed rail where possible to simplify travel.
5. Verify locally for seasonal access or reservation requirements.

## Common problems & fixes
- Itinerary overload: remove one day trip and add recovery time.
- Long transfers: rearrange the order to reduce backtracking.
- Weather changes: keep a flexible day for indoor activities.

## Checklist
- [ ] Three stop route selected.
- [ ] Transfers kept short.
- [ ] Rest days included.
- [ ] Seasonal access checked.
