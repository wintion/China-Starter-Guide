## Quick Answer
Winter can be cold and dry in the north and milder in the south. Pack layers and plan shorter outdoor days.

## Why this matters
Weather affects transport and sightseeing. A winter plan keeps you warm and flexible.

## Step-by-step
1. Check regional weather patterns for your cities and verify locally.
2. Pack layers, gloves, and a warm outer jacket.
3. Plan indoor alternatives for very cold days.
4. Allow extra time for transport and early sunsets.
5. Keep hot drinks and breaks in your schedule.

## Common problems & fixes
- Cold mornings slow your pace: start later and focus on indoor sights.
- Slippery conditions: wear shoes with good grip.
- Dry air discomfort: carry water and use moisturizers.

## Checklist
- [ ] Layered clothing packed.
- [ ] Indoor backup plan ready.
- [ ] Transport time buffer added.
- [ ] Weather updates checked.
