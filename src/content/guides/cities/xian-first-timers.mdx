## Quick Answer
Xian is compact and history focused. Stay inside or near the city wall for easy access.

## Why this matters
Most key sites are clustered, but day trips can take time. A central base helps keep days manageable.

## Step-by-step
1. Choose lodging near the city wall or central metro lines.
2. Plan one full day for the Terracotta Army trip and keep other days lighter.
3. Use the metro and short taxi rides for local movement.
4. Allocate time for the evening food streets without rushing.
5. Verify locally for attraction ticket rules.

## Common problems & fixes
- Day trip feels rushed: leave early and plan fewer stops.
- Crowds at major sites: go early and avoid weekends if possible.
- Confusing buses: use taxis or ride hailing for direct trips.

## Checklist
- [ ] Central area selected.
- [ ] Day trip planned with buffer.
- [ ] Local transport plan ready.
- [ ] Tickets confirmed.
