## Quick Answer
Yunnan is scenic and slower paced. Choose one or two base towns and avoid rushing between them.

## Why this matters
Distances between towns can be longer than expected, and altitude can affect pacing.

## Step-by-step
1. Choose a main entry city and one secondary town if time allows.
2. Plan slower mornings and reduce same day transfers.
3. Use trains or flights to reach the region, then local transport for day trips.
4. Stay near old towns or central areas for easier walking access.
5. Verify locally for altitude or weather considerations.

## Common problems & fixes
- Overplanning: cut one town and add rest time.
- Long transfer days: switch to a direct route or stay overnight.
- Weather shifts: keep flexible plans for outdoor days.

## Checklist
- [ ] One or two base towns selected.
- [ ] Transfer days minimized.
- [ ] Lodging near central areas chosen.
- [ ] Weather plan in place.
