## Quick Answer
Zhangjiajie requires early starts and steady walking. Stay near the park entrance for easier days.

## Why this matters
The park is large and can be tiring. A close base and a clear route plan makes visits easier.

## Step-by-step
1. Choose lodging near the main entrance to reduce travel time.
2. Plan one or two days in the park depending on your pace.
3. Start early to avoid crowds on key viewpoints.
4. Use internal transport and cable cars when available to save energy.
5. Verify locally for route closures or weather restrictions.

## Common problems & fixes
- Overly ambitious routes: shorten the plan and focus on key viewpoints.
- Crowded paths: visit early or later in the day.
- Weather changes: move to lower elevation routes.

## Checklist
- [ ] Base near entrance selected.
- [ ] Daily route planned with buffer.
- [ ] Early start scheduled.
- [ ] Weather plan ready.
