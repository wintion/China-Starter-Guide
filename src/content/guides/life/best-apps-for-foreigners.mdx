## Quick Answer
Install a small set of core apps for payments, transport, maps, and translation, then test them before you need them.

## Why this matters
Many daily tasks rely on apps. Having them ready prevents delays at the airport, hotel, or checkout.

## Step-by-step
1. List your core needs: payments, navigation, rides, and translation.
2. Install apps before departure and sign in where possible.
3. Open each app and locate the key screens you will use later.
4. Download offline maps and save your hotel address.
5. Verify locally if any app requires additional identity checks.

## Common problems & fixes
- App cannot install: try a different app store or update your OS.
- Sign in fails: reset your password and save it offline.
- App not working on arrival: check network access and try again later.

## Checklist
- [ ] Payment apps installed and verified.
- [ ] Maps and translation tools ready.
- [ ] Ride hailing app configured.
- [ ] Offline info saved.
