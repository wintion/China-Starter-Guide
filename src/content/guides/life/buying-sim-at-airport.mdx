## Quick Answer
Airport SIM counters are convenient but can be busier and more expensive. Bring your passport and confirm the plan details before paying.

## Why this matters
Your first hours often rely on data for transport and hotel check-in. A fast SIM setup keeps you moving.

## Step-by-step
1. Follow airport signs for SIM or telecom counters after arrival.
2. Compare plan duration, data size, and price.
3. Show your passport and allow staff to register the SIM.
4. Insert the SIM and confirm data works before leaving the counter.
5. Verify locally for any activation text or settings.

### Cautions
- Avoid giving out your passport to unofficial sellers.
- Confirm data limits and top-up rules before paying.
- Keep your home SIM safe for later use.

## Common problems & fixes
- No data after purchase: ask staff to check APN settings.
- SIM size mismatch: request the correct size or an adapter.
- Counter closed: buy a SIM in the city center or use eSIM.

## Checklist
- [ ] Passport ready for registration.
- [ ] Plan details confirmed.
- [ ] Data tested before leaving the counter.
- [ ] Home SIM stored safely.
