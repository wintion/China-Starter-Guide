## Quick Answer
Prepare a clear written note in Chinese for your dietary needs and show it before ordering.

## Why this matters
Ingredients can be hidden in sauces or broths. Clear communication reduces risk.

## Step-by-step
1. Write your dietary restrictions in Chinese and English.
2. Show the note to the staff before ordering.
3. Ask about sauces, broths, and marinades.
4. Choose simple dishes with visible ingredients.
5. Verify locally if you have severe allergies.

## Common problems & fixes
- Staff unsure: choose a simpler dish or a different restaurant.
- Hidden ingredients: ask about broths and sauces explicitly.
- Language barriers: use translation apps and show the note again.

## Checklist
- [ ] Written restriction note ready.
- [ ] Translation app available.
- [ ] Simple dish options identified.
- [ ] Emergency plan ready if needed.
