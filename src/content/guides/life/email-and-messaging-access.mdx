## Quick Answer
Assume some services may be slow or unavailable. Set up backups and keep important messages offline.

## Why this matters
You may need access to booking confirmations, banking alerts, or family messages.

## Step-by-step
1. Download copies of key confirmations and tickets.
2. Set up a secondary email account you can access easily.
3. Install at least one backup messaging app.
4. Test access on arrival and adjust your workflow.
5. Verify locally for any service restrictions.

### Cautions
- Avoid sending sensitive data on unsecured networks.
- Do not rely on a single inbox or messaging app.
- Keep backup recovery options updated.

## Common problems & fixes
- Login fails: use backup email or reset later on secure Wi-Fi.
- Messages delayed: switch networks or use a different app.
- App blocked: rely on offline confirmations and local contacts.

## Checklist
- [ ] Confirmations saved offline.
- [ ] Backup email set up.
- [ ] Secondary messaging app installed.
- [ ] Recovery options updated.
