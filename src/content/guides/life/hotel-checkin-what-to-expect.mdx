## Quick Answer
Expect to show your passport and booking confirmation. Some hotels may ask for additional registration details.

## Why this matters
Check-in is often the first official step after arrival. Being prepared prevents delays.

## Step-by-step
1. Present your passport and booking confirmation at the front desk.
2. Confirm room details and stay dates.
3. Complete any registration forms as requested.
4. Ask about Wi-Fi access, breakfast times, and checkout rules.
5. Verify locally for any additional policy requirements.

## Common problems & fixes
- Booking not found: show the confirmation number and email.
- Deposit requested: pay by card or cash and keep the receipt.
- Language barrier: use translation apps and show written details.

## Checklist
- [ ] Passport and booking confirmation ready.
- [ ] Payment method for deposit available.
- [ ] Wi-Fi details saved.
- [ ] Local address recorded.
