## Quick Answer
Some hotels have restrictions on foreign guests. Confirm acceptance before booking and keep proof in writing.

## Why this matters
Arriving to a hotel that cannot check you in creates stress and delays.

## Step-by-step
1. Check the booking listing for foreign guest acceptance.
2. Message the hotel to confirm they can accept your passport type.
3. Save written confirmation in case of disputes.
4. Keep a backup hotel option nearby.
5. Verify locally if policies change close to arrival.

## Common problems & fixes
- Hotel refuses check-in: show written confirmation or move to a backup hotel.
- Conflicting information online: contact the property directly for clarity.
- Late arrival: notify the hotel and confirm late check-in rules.

## Checklist
- [ ] Acceptance confirmed in writing.
- [ ] Backup hotel identified.
- [ ] Booking details saved offline.
- [ ] Arrival time communicated.
