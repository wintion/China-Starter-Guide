## Quick Answer
Point to photos, use translation apps, and keep a short set of food phrases ready.

## Why this matters
Food is a daily need. Simple tools help you order what you want and avoid confusion.

## Step-by-step
1. Look for picture menus or display dishes.
2. Use a translation app to read menu items and show your request.
3. Ask for spice level or ingredients with short phrases.
4. Confirm the final order by pointing or repeating the dish name.
5. Verify locally if you have allergies or dietary needs.

## Common problems & fixes
- No pictures available: use the camera translation and ask for recommendations.
- Wrong dish arrives: point to the menu item again and clarify politely.
- Spice level too strong: request a mild version next time.

## Checklist
- [ ] Translation app ready.
- [ ] Key food phrases saved.
- [ ] Allergy or dietary notes prepared.
- [ ] Payment method ready.
