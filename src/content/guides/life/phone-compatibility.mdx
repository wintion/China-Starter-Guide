## Quick Answer
Ensure your phone is unlocked and supports local bands. Test calling and data after activation.

## Why this matters
A locked or incompatible phone can leave you without data when you need it most.

## Step-by-step
1. Check if your phone is carrier unlocked.
2. Confirm your device supports local network bands.
3. Decide on SIM or eSIM based on compatibility.
4. After activation, place a test call and open a web page.
5. Verify locally if your device needs APN changes.

### Cautions
- Avoid factory resets or OS updates right before travel.
- Keep your home SIM for two factor verification if needed.
- Do not share device unlock codes with unknown sellers.

## Common problems & fixes
- No signal: toggle airplane mode and reselect the network.
- Data not working: update APN settings or contact the seller.
- Phone locked: contact your carrier before travel.

## Checklist
- [ ] Phone unlocked.
- [ ] Band support confirmed.
- [ ] SIM or eSIM selected.
- [ ] Test call and data completed.
