## Quick Answer
Some visitors must register their address with local authorities. Hotels often handle this, but verify locally.

## Why this matters
Missing registration can lead to problems during travel or checkout. A simple check avoids issues.

## Step-by-step
1. Ask your hotel if they handle registration for foreign guests.
2. If staying in a private rental, ask the host about registration steps.
3. Prepare your passport and entry documents for registration.
4. Complete registration within the required time window and verify locally.
5. Keep a record or receipt if provided.

## Common problems & fixes
- Host unsure of process: contact local authorities or switch to a hotel.
- Late registration: register as soon as possible and explain the delay.
- Missing documents: bring your passport and booking proof.

## Checklist
- [ ] Hotel or host confirmed registration steps.
- [ ] Passport ready.
- [ ] Time window noted.
- [ ] Registration proof saved.
