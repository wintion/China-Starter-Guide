## Quick Answer
Confirm the host can accept foreign guests, understand deposit terms, and clarify registration responsibilities.

## Why this matters
Rentals can differ from hotels in rules and support. Clear expectations reduce friction.

## Step-by-step
1. Confirm the host accepts foreign guests and ask about registration.
2. Review deposit, cancellation, and check-in rules carefully.
3. Save the address in Chinese and confirm the building access details.
4. Keep all messages and receipts in a secure folder.
5. Verify locally for any city specific registration rules.

## Common problems & fixes
- Host unresponsive on arrival: use a backup contact or switch to a hotel.
- Deposit dispute: keep photos and written records of the property condition.
- Registration unclear: ask the host to accompany you or choose a hotel.

## Checklist
- [ ] Host acceptance confirmed in writing.
- [ ] Deposit and cancellation rules understood.
- [ ] Address and access details saved.
- [ ] Registration steps clarified.
