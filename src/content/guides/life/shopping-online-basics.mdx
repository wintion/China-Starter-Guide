## Quick Answer
Use a trusted shopping app, set your delivery address carefully, and track orders in app.

## Why this matters
Online shopping is convenient, but address formatting and delivery steps can be confusing.

## Step-by-step
1. Choose a shopping app and sign in with your phone number.
2. Set your delivery address in Chinese and add building or room details.
3. Select items, confirm prices, and place the order.
4. Track delivery in the app and watch for courier messages.
5. Verify locally if the courier needs a pickup point.

## Common problems & fixes
- Address not accepted: ask hotel staff for the correct format.
- Courier cannot reach you: provide a landmark and stay reachable.
- Payment fails: use a backup wallet or card.

## Checklist
- [ ] Address saved in Chinese.
- [ ] Delivery instructions added.
- [ ] Payment method ready.
- [ ] Courier contact enabled.
