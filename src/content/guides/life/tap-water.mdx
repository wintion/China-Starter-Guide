## Quick Answer
Use bottled or boiled water for drinking. Use tap water for washing unless advised otherwise.

## Why this matters
Water habits vary by region and accommodation. A simple plan avoids stomach issues.

## Step-by-step
1. Check your hotel guidance on drinking water.
2. Use bottled water for drinking and brushing teeth if unsure.
3. If boiling water, use a clean kettle and let it cool.
4. Carry a refillable bottle for day trips.
5. Verify locally for any specific advisories.

## Common problems & fixes
- Unclear guidance: ask hotel staff and use bottled water by default.
- Bottle sizes too large: buy smaller bottles for daily use.
- Running out during the day: buy water at convenience stores.

## Checklist
- [ ] Bottled water available.
- [ ] Hotel guidance checked.
- [ ] Refillable bottle packed.
- [ ] Day trip water plan ready.
