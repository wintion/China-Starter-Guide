## Quick Answer
Use text translation for short messages and camera translation for menus and signs.

## Why this matters
Language gaps are common. Quick translation reduces friction in restaurants and transit.

## Step-by-step
1. Download offline language packs before travel.
2. Save common phrases such as hotel address and dietary needs.
3. Use camera mode for signs, menus, and labels.
4. Keep translations short and confirm with a map or context.
5. Verify locally if the translation seems unclear.

## Common problems & fixes
- Translation inaccurate: try shorter text or reframe the image.
- App fails offline: reopen and ensure offline packs are downloaded.
- Staff does not understand: show the translation in large text.

## Checklist
- [ ] Offline language pack downloaded.
- [ ] Common phrases saved.
- [ ] Camera translation tested.
- [ ] Backup paper notes ready.
