## Quick Answer
VPN access can be inconsistent. Have backup ways to access essential services and keep expectations realistic.

## Why this matters
Connectivity affects maps, email, and work tools. A practical plan reduces disruption.

## Step-by-step
1. Identify the services you must access while traveling.
2. Prepare alternative apps or offline copies of key information.
3. Test your setup before travel and note any required steps.
4. If you use a VPN, set it up in advance and verify locally for access.
5. Keep a fallback plan in case it stops working.

### Cautions
- Rules and access conditions can change without notice.
- Avoid relying on a single connectivity method for critical tasks.
- Do not share account details with untrusted providers.

## Common problems & fixes
- Connection drops: switch networks or try again later.
- App cannot authenticate: use a backup service or offline data.
- Setup fails on arrival: wait for stable connectivity and recheck settings.

## Checklist
- [ ] Critical services identified.
- [ ] Backup apps installed.
- [ ] Offline copies saved.
- [ ] Fallback plan ready.
