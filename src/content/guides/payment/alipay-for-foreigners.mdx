## Quick Answer
Alipay can work with foreign cards, but you must complete verification and add a card before you can pay reliably.

## Why this matters
Alipay is widely accepted for daily spending. A clean setup saves time when you are in line or under pressure to pay quickly.

## Step-by-step
1. Open Alipay and create an account with your phone number.
2. Look for identity verification in Profile or Settings and complete the passport steps. If the screens differ, verify locally.
3. Go to Wallet or Cards and tap Add card. Enter your card details and follow any verification prompts.
4. Test the wallet with a small purchase so you know it is working before you need it.
5. At checkout, open Alipay and tap Scan to scan the merchant QR code, or tap Pay to show your code.

## Common problems & fixes
- Verification pending: wait for the review to finish and avoid repeated submissions.
- Card add fails: try another card, or contact your bank to allow the transaction.
- Payment blocked: switch to cash or a backup wallet and try again later.

## Checklist
- [ ] Account created and verified.
- [ ] Foreign card added and confirmed.
- [ ] Scan and Pay screens located.
- [ ] Backup payment ready.
