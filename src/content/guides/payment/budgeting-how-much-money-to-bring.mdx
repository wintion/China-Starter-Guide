## Quick Answer
Budget based on your trip style, then add a buffer. Split funds across wallet, card, and cash.

## Why this matters
Costs vary by city and season. A flexible plan prevents shortages without carrying excessive cash.

## Step-by-step
1. List your major categories: lodging, transport, food, and activities.
2. Estimate each category with conservative assumptions and verify locally for time-sensitive areas.
3. Add a buffer for unexpected costs like delays or changes.
4. Keep most funds in your wallet or card, with a smaller cash reserve.
5. Open your wallet app, tap Bills or Transactions, and note your daily totals. Labels can change, so verify locally.
6. Track spending daily and adjust as you learn actual costs.

## Common problems & fixes
- Budget feels too tight: scale back optional activities or add a buffer.
- Too much cash on hand: deposit back to your card or keep it secured.
- Daily costs unclear: track a few days and recalibrate.

## Checklist
- [ ] Categories listed and estimated.
- [ ] Buffer added for flexibility.
- [ ] Funds split across payment methods.
- [ ] Spending tracked daily.
