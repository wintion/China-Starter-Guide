## Quick Answer
Cash is accepted but not always preferred. Carry small notes for small shops, markets, or when a wallet fails.

## Why this matters
Some vendors are cash-only, and cash can save you in areas with weak connectivity or wallet issues.

## Step-by-step
1. Withdraw cash after arrival or exchange a modest amount beforehand.
2. Break large notes at supermarkets or convenience stores.
3. Keep cash separate from your phone and cards for security.
4. Open your wallet app and check recent payments so you do not double-pay. Verify locally if labels differ.
5. Pay with cash when the merchant requests it or when QR codes are not accepted.

## Common problems & fixes
- No small change: make a small purchase to get change and keep it for later.
- Cash refused: politely ask again, but switch to wallet or card if needed.
- Lost cash: reduce risk by splitting your cash across pockets.

## Checklist
- [ ] Small notes ready for small purchases.
- [ ] Wallet app as primary payment.
- [ ] Backup card in a separate place.
- [ ] Plan for cash-only vendors.
