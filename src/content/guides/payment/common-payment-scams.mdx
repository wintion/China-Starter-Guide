## Quick Answer
Only pay through trusted apps, confirm the merchant name, and avoid scanning unofficial codes.

## Why this matters
Most payments are safe, but a few common tricks can lead to wrong recipients or fake charges.

## Step-by-step
1. Use your wallet app to scan merchant codes and confirm the merchant name.
2. Avoid paying through links or unknown QR codes in public areas.
3. In taxis, scan the official driver code and confirm the amount.
4. Keep receipts for higher-value payments.
5. If something looks off, cancel and verify locally with the staff.

## Common problems & fixes
- Merchant name looks wrong: cancel and request the official code.
- QR code taped over: ask staff to provide the official code.
- Unexpected amount: do not confirm and ask for clarification.

## Checklist
- [ ] Merchant name confirmed in app.
- [ ] Amount verified before payment.
- [ ] Receipts saved for larger purchases.
- [ ] Backup payment method ready.
