## Quick answer
Most visitors rely on mobile wallets for daily purchases, but you should carry some cash and a backup card for hotels and transport.

## What works now
### Mobile wallets
- Alipay and WeChat Pay can be linked to some foreign cards.
- Identity checks are common, so allow time for verification.

### Cards
- Larger hotels, airports, and big chains accept Visa or Mastercard.
- Smaller shops often do not support foreign cards.

### Cash
- Still accepted at markets, taxis, and smaller restaurants.
- Keep small notes; change is not always available.

## Set up before arrival
1. Install Alipay and WeChat Pay and add a payment method.
2. Prepare a passport scan for verification.
3. Turn on bank alerts for international charges.

## Top-up and limits
Wallets have daily limits and may require a local phone number for higher caps. Start with a small top-up and confirm it works.

## Common pitfalls
- Assuming every taxi accepts cards or QR codes.
- Waiting until arrival to set up verification.
- Relying on a single payment method.
