## Quick Answer
You can often link a foreign card by adding it inside the wallet app, completing identity checks, and confirming a verification prompt.

## Why this matters
If a card is not linked correctly, payments can fail at the worst time. A clean setup gives you a reliable primary method.

## Step-by-step
1. Open your wallet app and find Wallet, Cards, or Payment Methods.
2. Tap Add card and enter card details exactly as they appear on the card.
3. Complete identity verification with your passport if prompted. Verify locally if screens change.
4. Approve any bank verification request or small test charge.
5. Save the card and confirm it appears in your wallet list.

## Common problems & fixes
- Verification SMS never arrives: check roaming, or ask your bank for alternate verification.
- Card rejected: try another card type or contact the bank to allow wallet transactions.
- Card added but payments fail: remove and re-add the card, then test with a small purchase.

## Checklist
- [ ] Passport details ready for verification.
- [ ] Bank notified of travel and wallet usage.
- [ ] Card added and visible in wallet.
- [ ] Backup card or cash ready.
