## Quick Answer
Most cities support transit QR codes inside Alipay or WeChat. Activate the city transit card, then scan at gates.

## Why this matters
Metro and bus rides are frequent and time-sensitive. A ready transit QR code avoids delays at the gate.

## Step-by-step
1. Open your wallet and find Transport or Transit. The label may vary, so verify locally.
2. Choose your city and accept the terms to activate the transit card.
3. Tap the transit card to display the QR code.
4. Scan the QR at metro gates or bus readers and wait for confirmation.
5. If the scanner fails, try again with higher brightness or use a physical ticket.

## Common problems & fixes
- City not listed: use a physical ticket or local transit app.
- Code loads slowly: switch to mobile data or reopen the app.
- Payment fails: check your default payment method in the wallet.

## Checklist
- [ ] Transit card activated for the current city.
- [ ] QR code loads quickly.
- [ ] Backup card or cash ready.
- [ ] Gate accepted the scan.
