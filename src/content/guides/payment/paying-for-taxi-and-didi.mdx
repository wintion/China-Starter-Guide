## Quick Answer
In ride-hailing apps, payment happens after the ride. In street taxis, you usually scan a QR code or show your pay code.

## Why this matters
Transport payments can be rushed. Knowing the flow helps you avoid mistakes and confirm the fare.

## Step-by-step
1. For ride-hailing, confirm your pickup, vehicle, and fare estimate in the app.
2. After the ride, open the app payment screen and confirm the final amount.
3. For street taxis, ask for the payment code. Scan the QR or show your pay code.
4. Wait for the in-app confirmation and keep a screenshot if needed.
5. If the screen labels differ, follow the on-screen prompts and verify locally.

## Common problems & fixes
- Driver QR code does not scan: increase brightness or ask to enter the amount manually.
- App shows a different fare: check the trip details before paying.
- Payment pending: stay on the payment screen until confirmation appears.

## Checklist
- [ ] Vehicle details confirmed before entering.
- [ ] Payment screen shows the correct amount.
- [ ] Receipt saved if needed.
- [ ] Backup payment ready.
