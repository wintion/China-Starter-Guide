## Quick Answer
Pause, check your connection and balance, then retry with a different method. Keep a backup option ready.

## Why this matters
Payment failures are stressful at checkout. A calm, repeatable process helps you recover quickly.

## Step-by-step
1. Confirm you have signal or Wi-Fi. Switch networks if needed.
2. Re-open the wallet app and check for pending verification steps.
3. Confirm the amount and currency on the payment screen.
4. Try the alternative flow: scan the merchant code instead of showing your code, or vice versa.
5. Switch to a backup method such as cash or another wallet.

## Common problems & fixes
- Risk control prompt: wait a few minutes, then try again or pay with cash.
- Card verification expired: remove and re-add the card, then test a small payment.
- Merchant system down: ask for another QR code or pay at a different counter.

## Checklist
- [ ] Stable connection confirmed.
- [ ] Wallet verification complete.
- [ ] Backup method available.
- [ ] Receipts checked for duplicates.
