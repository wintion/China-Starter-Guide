## Quick Answer
You will either scan a merchant QR code or show your own QR code to the cashier. Both are common.

## Why this matters
Understanding the two flows helps you pay faster and avoid confusion at checkout.

## Step-by-step
1. For scan-to-pay: open your wallet, tap Scan, and scan the merchant code.
2. Enter the amount if required and confirm the merchant name before paying.
3. For show-to-pay: open the wallet and tap Pay to display your code.
4. Let the cashier scan your screen and wait for the confirmation message.
5. If the flow looks different, follow in-app prompts and verify locally.

## Common problems & fixes
- Code does not scan: increase brightness or clean the screen.
- Wrong merchant name: cancel immediately and ask for the correct code.
- Payment pending: keep the screen open and wait for a success notice.

## Checklist
- [ ] Scan and Pay buttons located.
- [ ] Screen brightness set high.
- [ ] Merchant name checked before confirming.
- [ ] Backup method ready.
