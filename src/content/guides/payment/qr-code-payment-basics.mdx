## Quick Answer

There are **two different QR payment flows** in China.  
You must use the **correct one for the counter**, or the payment will fail.  
Most problems happen because travelers use the wrong flow—not because their card or wallet is broken.

---

## The Core Reality (Most Important Concept)

In China, QR payments work in **two opposite directions**:

1. **You scan the merchant’s QR**
2. **The merchant scans your QR**

These are **not interchangeable**.

Using the wrong one causes:
- Failed payments
- Awkward delays
- Double charges
- Confused cashiers

---

## The Two QR Payment Flows (Understand This Once)

### Flow A: You Scan the Merchant QR
- You open **Scan**
- You scan a printed QR at the counter
- You confirm amount and pay

**Common in:**
- Small shops
- Street food
- Cafes
- Informal counters

---

### Flow B: Merchant Scans Your QR
- You open **Pay**
- A moving QR appears on your phone
- Cashier scans your screen

**Common in:**
- Supermarkets
- Convenience stores
- Metro gates
- Chain restaurants

---

## Why Using the Wrong Flow Fails

If you:
- Try to scan when the cashier expects your code
- Show your code when the system expects you to scan

The terminal simply **does nothing**.

This looks like a technical failure—but it is not.

---

## Exact Actions: How to Choose the Right Flow Instantly

### Rule 1: Look at the counter, not the app
- **Printed QR on counter?** → You scan.
- **Scanner gun or glass window?** → They scan you.

Ignore what you *prefer* to do.

---

### Rule 2: Watch the cashier’s body language
- Cashier holds a scanner → show your **Pay** code
- Cashier points at a QR → open **Scan**

They will not explain verbally.

---

### Rule 3: If nothing happens in 3 seconds, stop
- Cancel the action
- Switch to the other flow
- Do not keep retrying

Retrying causes duplicates.

---

## Common QR Payment Mistakes

- Opening the wrong screen
- Scanning personal (non-merchant) QR codes
- Retrying while payment is “processing”
- Letting screen brightness stay low
- Blocking the QR with notifications

Most failures are **operator errors**, not system errors.

---

## Failure Scenarios & Fixes

- **Cashier waits and nothing happens**: you chose the wrong flow—switch.
- **Scan opens a transfer page**: wrong QR—cancel immediately.
- **Payment shows processing**: stop and check bills before retrying.
- **Scanner cannot read your screen**: increase brightness and hold steady.
- **Crowd pressure builds**: step aside and reset calmly.

Speed comes from correctness, not urgency.

---

## Reality Check

- Locals switch flows instantly.
- No one explains the difference in line.
- Using the wrong flow is normal for newcomers.
- Correcting quickly is what matters.

This is a skill, not knowledge.

---

## What Locals Do Instead

- They read the counter setup first.
- They cancel immediately if nothing happens.
- They never retry blindly.
- They switch wallets or methods without drama.

Copy this behavior.

---

## Checklist

- [ ] Identify who scans whom.
- [ ] Open **Scan** or **Pay** accordingly.
- [ ] Screen brightness high.
- [ ] Cancel immediately if stalled.
- [ ] Check bills before retrying.
