## Quick Answer
One person can pay the merchant, then send or request transfers from others in the wallet app.

## Why this matters
Group meals and shared rides are common. Clear payment flows reduce confusion and help everyone pay fairly.

## Step-by-step
1. Decide who will pay the merchant at checkout.
2. Open your wallet and find Transfer, Send, or Request. Labels can change, so verify locally.
3. Select a contact or scan a personal QR code.
4. Enter the amount and add a note for clarity.
5. Confirm the transfer and wait for the in-app success message.

## Common problems & fixes
- Contact not found: use a QR code or ask them to send their code.
- Transfer blocked: check verification settings or try a smaller amount.
- Wrong recipient: cancel immediately if possible and contact support.

## Checklist
- [ ] Agreed on who pays first.
- [ ] Correct recipient confirmed.
- [ ] Transfer note added.
- [ ] Confirmation message saved.
