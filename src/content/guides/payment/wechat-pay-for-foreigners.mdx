## Quick Answer
WeChat Pay can accept foreign cards after verification. Complete setup before you rely on it for daily purchases.

## Why this matters
Many merchants and services expect WeChat Pay. A verified wallet avoids delays at checkout and in-app purchases.

## Step-by-step
1. Open WeChat and create an account with your phone number.
2. Tap Me, then Services or Pay to open the wallet area. Labels can change, so verify locally.
3. Go to Wallet or Cards and add your foreign card. Follow verification prompts and set a payment password.
4. In the main wallet screen, locate Scan and Pay so you can access them quickly.
5. Test with a small purchase and confirm you receive a payment confirmation in the app.

## Common problems & fixes
- Wallet not visible: update the app or check region settings.
- Card verification fails: confirm your bank allows overseas wallet usage.
- Payment rejected: try another method or complete any pending identity checks.

## Checklist
- [ ] WeChat account verified.
- [ ] Card added and payment password set.
- [ ] Scan and Pay buttons located.
- [ ] Backup payment option ready.
