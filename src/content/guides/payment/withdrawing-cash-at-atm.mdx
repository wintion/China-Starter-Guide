## Quick Answer
Use bank ATMs in well-lit locations, select English if available, and keep receipts. Verify limits and fees locally.

## Why this matters
ATMs are a dependable backup when wallets fail, but fees and limits can vary by bank and card.

## Step-by-step
1. Choose a bank ATM inside a branch, mall, or airport.
2. Insert your card, select a language, and choose withdrawal.
3. Select the account type requested and enter your amount.
4. Confirm the fee prompt if shown, then collect cash, card, and receipt.
5. Open your banking or wallet app and confirm the withdrawal appears. Verify locally if labels differ.
6. Store the receipt until the transaction posts.

## Common problems & fixes
- Card not accepted: try another ATM or bank network.
- Machine keeps card: contact the bank branch immediately if open.
- Cash not dispensed: keep the receipt and contact your bank.

## Checklist
- [ ] ATM chosen in a safe, monitored area.
- [ ] Card and PIN ready.
- [ ] Receipt saved for reconciliation.
- [ ] Backup payment ready in case of failure.
