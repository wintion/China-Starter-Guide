## Quick Answer
Be polite, stay patient, and follow local cues. Small gestures and calm behavior go a long way.

## Why this matters
First impressions affect how quickly you get help or service. Clear, respectful behavior smooths daily interactions.

## Step-by-step
1. Greet politely and use a calm tone in shops or hotels.
2. Keep your voice moderate in public spaces.
3. Follow queue behavior and allow others to pass in tight spaces.
4. Offer and receive items with both hands when appropriate.
5. Verify locally for venue specific etiquette.

## Common problems & fixes
- You are unsure of a custom: observe locals and follow their lead.
- A situation feels awkward: smile, apologize, and move on.
- You offend someone accidentally: keep calm and clarify your intent.

## Checklist
- [ ] Calm and polite tone ready.
- [ ] Queue behavior observed.
- [ ] Small courtesy phrases prepared.
- [ ] Willingness to follow local cues.
