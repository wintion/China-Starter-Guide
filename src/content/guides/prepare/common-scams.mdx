## Quick Answer
Most scams are avoidable by using official services, confirming prices, and walking away when pressured.

## Why this matters
Scams can be stressful and time consuming. Knowing the patterns helps you respond quickly and safely.

## Step-by-step
1. Use official taxi stands, ticket counters, and verified apps.
2. Confirm prices before agreeing to tours, rides, or services.
3. Avoid unsolicited offers that rush you or demand upfront payment.
4. Keep your wallet and phone secure in crowded areas.
5. Verify locally for common scams in your arrival city.

## Common problems & fixes
- You are pressured to follow someone: decline and keep walking to a public area.
- A QR code looks unofficial: ask for the official code or pay another way.
- A price suddenly changes: stop the transaction and walk away.

## Checklist
- [ ] Official services used when possible.
- [ ] Prices confirmed before paying.
- [ ] Wallet and phone secured.
- [ ] Trusted payment method ready.
