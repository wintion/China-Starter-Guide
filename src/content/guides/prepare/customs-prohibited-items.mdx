## Quick Answer
Customs rules can change. Check prohibited and restricted items before packing and declare items when required.

## Why this matters
Unexpected items can lead to delays or confiscation. A quick review prevents problems at arrival.

## Step-by-step
1. Review current customs rules for prohibited and restricted items.
2. Remove any questionable items before packing.
3. Keep receipts for expensive items in case you need to declare them.
4. Use the declaration channel if you are unsure.
5. Verify locally for any updates or special categories.

## Common problems & fixes
- Item flagged at inspection: cooperate and provide information.
- Unsure whether to declare: choose to declare and ask for guidance.
- Conflicting advice: rely on official customs rules and verify locally.

## Checklist
- [ ] Prohibited items list checked.
- [ ] Receipts packed for high value items.
- [ ] Declaration plan ready.
- [ ] Packing adjusted for compliance.
