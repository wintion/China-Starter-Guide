## Quick Answer
Drone rules vary by city and zone. Confirm registration and no fly areas before you pack or fly.

## Why this matters
Drones are regulated and may require registration. Flying in restricted zones can lead to serious issues.

## Step-by-step
1. Check whether your drone requires registration for your trip.
2. Review local no fly zones and airport restrictions.
3. Pack your drone safely and carry batteries per airline guidance.
4. Fly only in permitted areas and keep flight records if required.
5. Verify locally for current rules, as restrictions can change.

## Common problems & fixes
- Drone stopped at customs: provide receipts and comply with instructions.
- Unclear flying zones: do not fly until you confirm local rules.
- Battery issues on flights: follow airline battery guidance and keep batteries in carry on.

## Checklist
- [ ] Registration requirements checked.
- [ ] No fly zones reviewed.
- [ ] Batteries packed safely.
- [ ] Flight plan confirmed.
