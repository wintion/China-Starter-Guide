## Quick Answer
Store key contacts offline, keep your passport accessible, and know how to reach local help if needed.

## Why this matters
In emergencies, quick access to contacts and documents saves time and reduces stress.

## Step-by-step
1. Save emergency numbers and your embassy contact in your phone and on paper.
2. Keep a photo of your passport and visa page in a secure offline folder.
3. Share your itinerary and hotel address with a trusted contact.
4. Identify a nearby clinic or hospital and verify locally for hours.
5. Keep insurance details and policy numbers accessible.

## Common problems & fixes
- Phone lost: use your paper list and hotel staff for assistance.
- Language barrier: show a written note with your issue and address.
- Time sensitive help needed: call local emergency services and stay in place.

## Checklist
- [ ] Emergency numbers saved offline.
- [ ] Passport copy stored securely.
- [ ] Embassy contact recorded.
- [ ] Insurance details accessible.
