## Quick Answer
English support is mixed. Prepare key phrases, use translation tools, and keep addresses in Chinese.

## Why this matters
Clear communication saves time when booking hotels, ordering transport, or asking for help.

## Step-by-step
1. Save your hotel address and key destinations in Chinese.
2. Download an offline translation tool and practice basic phrases.
3. Use simple, polite English and short sentences.
4. Show written Chinese text when speaking is difficult.
5. Verify locally if place names differ from translations.

## Common problems & fixes
- Staff do not understand: show the written address or map pin.
- Translation app fails: use offline mode or a printed phrase list.
- Miscommunication about prices: confirm the amount before paying.

## Checklist
- [ ] Chinese addresses saved.
- [ ] Translation tool ready offline.
- [ ] Basic phrases prepared.
- [ ] Map pins verified.
