## Quick Answer
Carry your passport, visa or entry status proof, and proof of onward travel and lodging.

## Why this matters
Airlines and immigration officers may request documents before boarding and on arrival.

## Step-by-step
1. Prepare your passport and entry status proof (visa or visa free eligibility).
2. Print or save your onward ticket and hotel confirmation.
3. Keep digital and paper copies in separate places.
4. Store key documents in an easy to access folder.
5. Verify locally for any special entry requirements.

## Common problems & fixes
- Airline requests extra proof: show return ticket and hotel confirmation.
- Document mismatch: confirm names match your passport exactly.
- Lost documents: use backups and contact your embassy if needed.

## Checklist
- [ ] Passport ready and valid.
- [ ] Visa or entry proof prepared.
- [ ] Onward travel proof saved.
- [ ] Hotel confirmation accessible.
