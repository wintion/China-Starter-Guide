## Quick Answer
Health requirements can change. Be ready to provide basic travel and health information if requested.

## Why this matters
Some entry points require a health declaration. Being prepared avoids delays at arrival.

## Step-by-step
1. Check current health entry requirements before your flight.
2. Keep your travel itinerary and hotel details accessible.
3. If a form or app is required, complete it before arrival and save the confirmation.
4. Be prepared for temperature checks or basic screening.
5. Verify locally for updated requirements before departure.

## Common problems & fixes
- App not working: use a browser version if available or ask staff for help.
- Missing confirmation: take screenshots and keep them offline.
- Incomplete info: update your itinerary details and resubmit.

## Checklist
- [ ] Health requirements checked.
- [ ] Itinerary details ready.
- [ ] Form or app completed if required.
- [ ] Confirmation saved offline.
