## Quick Answer
Some sites and services may be restricted. Prepare alternative apps and offline resources before you arrive.

## Why this matters
Connectivity affects maps, payments, and communication. A backup plan prevents confusion.

## Step-by-step
1. Download offline maps and key documents before travel.
2. Install alternative apps for messaging and email.
3. Save critical information locally, including addresses and booking details.
4. Test your apps on arrival and adjust your workflow.
5. Verify locally for any changes or access issues.

## Common problems & fixes
- A site does not load: use a local alternative or offline copy.
- App login fails: check your network settings and try again later.
- You cannot access email: keep a backup account or offline notes.

## Checklist
- [ ] Offline maps downloaded.
- [ ] Backup apps installed.
- [ ] Critical info saved locally.
- [ ] Connectivity plan confirmed.
