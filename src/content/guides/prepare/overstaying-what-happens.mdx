## Quick Answer
Overstaying can lead to fines or travel issues. Track your allowed stay and exit on time.

## Why this matters
Even short overstays can create complications for future travel. A clear plan prevents mistakes.

## Step-by-step
1. Confirm your allowed stay based on your entry status and verify locally.
2. Mark your latest exit day on a calendar and set reminders.
3. Keep a buffer day to handle delays or schedule changes.
4. If you need more time, research extension options early.
5. If you are already late, seek official guidance immediately.

## Common problems & fixes
- Miscounted days: review your entry stamp and correct your plan.
- Flight canceled: rebook quickly and keep documentation of the disruption.
- Extension unclear: ask official services for guidance before your deadline.

## Checklist
- [ ] Allowed stay confirmed.
- [ ] Exit date set with buffer.
- [ ] Reminders scheduled.
- [ ] Extension plan ready if needed.
