## Quick Answer
Many airlines require proof of onward travel. Keep a confirmed booking ready in print or offline.

## Why this matters
Airlines can deny boarding without proper proof. A simple preparation avoids last minute stress.

## Step-by-step
1. Book an onward ticket that matches your planned exit.
2. Save the confirmation with your name and date clearly visible.
3. Keep a printed copy and an offline screenshot.
4. Show the proof at check in if requested.
5. Verify locally for any carrier specific requirements.

## Common problems & fixes
- Airline cannot find your booking: show the confirmation number and screenshot.
- Ticket date mismatch: rebook to match your allowed stay.
- Lost confirmation: retrieve it from your email or booking account.

## Checklist
- [ ] Onward ticket booked.
- [ ] Confirmation saved offline.
- [ ] Printout packed.
- [ ] Details match passport name.
