## Who qualifies
Visa-free rules vary by passport and purpose of visit. Always check the latest policy before booking.

## Allowed activities
Tourism and short business visits are typical. Work and long-term study are not covered.

## Length of stay
The allowed length depends on the policy and entry point. Count days carefully and include your arrival day.

### Transit vs visa-free
Transit-without-visa programs have stricter routing rules and usually require onward tickets.

## Documents to carry
- Passport valid for at least six months.
- Onward ticket showing departure from China.
- Hotel booking or invitation letter.

## Arrival checklist
Keep paper copies of key documents in case your phone has no signal at immigration.

## Common pitfalls
- Assuming visa-free applies to all entry points.
- Booking a one-way ticket without proof of onward travel.
- Overstaying by counting days incorrectly.
