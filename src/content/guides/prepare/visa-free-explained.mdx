## Quick Answer
Visa free policies depend on passport and route. Check eligibility and required documents before booking.

## Why this matters
Eligibility rules change and can vary by entry point. Confirming early prevents denied boarding.

## Step-by-step
1. Check official policy for your passport and planned entry point.
2. Confirm allowed length of stay and any transit conditions.
3. Prepare supporting documents such as onward tickets and hotel bookings.
4. Keep printed copies of key documents for check in and arrival.
5. Verify locally for the latest policy updates before departure.

## Common problems & fixes
- Airline says you need a visa: show the official policy and ask for a supervisor.
- Entry point not covered: switch routes or apply for a visa.
- Documentation incomplete: prepare proof of onward travel and lodging.

## Checklist
- [ ] Passport eligibility confirmed.
- [ ] Entry point and route verified.
- [ ] Onward travel proof ready.
- [ ] Printed documents packed.
