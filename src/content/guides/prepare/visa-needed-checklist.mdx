## Quick Answer
If you are not eligible for visa free entry, gather documents early and confirm requirements with official sources.

## Why this matters
Visa requirements can change and may take time. Preparing early reduces last minute stress.

## Step-by-step
1. Confirm whether your passport requires a visa for your trip type.
2. Check official requirements for photos, forms, and appointments.
3. Prepare proof of travel, accommodation, and itinerary.
4. Ensure your passport validity meets the required window.
5. Verify locally for any updates before submitting.

## Common problems & fixes
- Missing document: reschedule and bring the required proof.
- Photo rejected: use a photo service that follows official specs.
- Processing delays: adjust your travel date or request expedited handling if available.

## Checklist
- [ ] Visa requirement confirmed.
- [ ] Forms completed and saved.
- [ ] Photos meet official specs.
- [ ] Itinerary and lodging proof ready.
