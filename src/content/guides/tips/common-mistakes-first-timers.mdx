## Quick Answer
Most mistakes come from rushing: relying on one payment method, not saving addresses, and packing too many city transfers.

## Why this matters
Fixing these issues on the spot costs time and energy. A little prep makes the trip smoother.

## Step-by-step
1. Set up two payment methods and test them on a small purchase.
2. Save your hotel address in the local language and keep a printed copy.
3. Limit city changes and keep travel days light.
4. Download offline maps and key confirmations.
5. Verify locally for any policy or schedule changes.

## Common problems & fixes
- Payment fails: switch to your backup method or cash.
- You cannot explain your destination: show the saved address or map pin.
- You feel rushed: drop one activity and add a rest block.

## Checklist
- [ ] Two payment methods ready.
- [ ] Address saved and printed.
- [ ] Offline maps downloaded.
- [ ] Buffer time in the itinerary.
