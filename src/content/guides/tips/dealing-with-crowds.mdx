## Quick Answer
Plan early starts, keep your belongings secure, and stay patient in busy areas.

## Why this matters
Crowds can slow you down and increase the risk of losing items or missing transport.

## Step-by-step
1. Visit major sights early to avoid peak crowds.
2. Keep your bag in front and your phone secured.
3. Use clear meeting points if you are traveling with others.
4. Allow extra time for ticket lines and security checks.
5. Verify locally for crowd control measures at popular sites.

## Common problems & fixes
- You feel overwhelmed: step aside to a quieter area and regroup.
- Items feel unsafe: move valuables to inner pockets.
- You are separated from your group: meet at a pre-set landmark.

## Checklist
- [ ] Early start planned.
- [ ] Meeting point agreed.
- [ ] Valuables secured.
- [ ] Extra time added for queues.
