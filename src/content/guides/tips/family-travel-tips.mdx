## Quick Answer
Build in breaks, choose direct transport, and keep daily plans flexible.

## Why this matters
Families move at different speeds. A flexible plan reduces stress and keeps everyone comfortable.

## Step-by-step
1. Choose lodging near transport and food options.
2. Plan one main activity per day and add rest breaks.
3. Use taxis or ride hailing for shorter, direct trips.
4. Keep snacks, water, and a small first aid kit handy.
5. Verify locally for family services such as elevators and restrooms.

## Common problems & fixes
- Missed transport: rebook the next train and adjust the day plan.
- Kids tire quickly: shorten visits and add quiet time.
- Crowded areas: use quieter entrances or visit early.

## Checklist
- [ ] Lodging near transport selected.
- [ ] Breaks scheduled into the day.
- [ ] Snacks and water packed.
- [ ] Backup transport ready.
