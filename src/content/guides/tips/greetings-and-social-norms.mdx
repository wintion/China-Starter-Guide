## Quick Answer
A polite greeting, a small nod, and a calm tone are usually enough. Use titles if you know them.

## Why this matters
Good first impressions make daily interactions easier and more comfortable.

## Step-by-step
1. Greet with a brief "Hello" and a friendly nod or smile.
2. Use respectful titles if you know them, or use "Sir" or "Maam".
3. Keep the greeting short and move to your request.
4. If someone hands you a document or card, accept it carefully and thank them.
5. Verify locally for formal settings such as hotels or business meetings.

## Common problems & fixes
- You forget a greeting: start with "Excuse me" and continue.
- You are unsure of a title: use a polite neutral greeting.
- You feel rushed: keep it brief and clear.

## Checklist
- [ ] Polite greeting ready.
- [ ] Calm tone used.
- [ ] Thanks expressed when receiving help.
- [ ] Simple follow-up request prepared.
