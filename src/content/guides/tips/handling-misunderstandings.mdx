## Quick Answer
Pause, restate your request simply, and use visuals or translation tools. Stay calm and polite.

## Why this matters
Misunderstandings happen frequently in travel. A simple process keeps things friendly.

## Step-by-step
1. Pause and take a breath before repeating your request.
2. Use simple English and short sentences.
3. Show a written note or a translated message.
4. Use photos or a map pin to clarify.
5. Verify locally for any rules or constraints you might have missed.

## Common problems & fixes
- Staff look confused: try a shorter request and show a visual.
- You feel frustrated: pause and try again or ask another person.
- You receive the wrong item: thank them and clarify again.

## Checklist
- [ ] Short request prepared.
- [ ] Written note or translation ready.
- [ ] Visual aid available.
- [ ] Calm tone maintained.
