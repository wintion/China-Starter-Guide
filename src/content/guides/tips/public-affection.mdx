## Quick Answer
Public comfort levels can vary. Keep affection low key in public spaces and follow local cues.

## Why this matters
Respecting local norms helps you avoid awkward moments and keeps interactions smooth.

## Step-by-step
1. Observe the setting and match the tone of the space.
2. Keep public affection brief and low key in crowded areas.
3. Avoid blocking walkways or lingering in tight spaces.
4. If you are unsure, choose a more private setting.
5. Verify locally for venue specific expectations.

## Common problems & fixes
- You feel uncomfortable in a crowded area: move to a quieter place.
- Someone reacts negatively: adjust your behavior and move on.
- Unclear norms: follow the most conservative behavior in the area.

## Checklist
- [ ] Local cues observed.
- [ ] Low key behavior in public spaces.
- [ ] Respect for shared areas.
- [ ] Willingness to adjust quickly.
