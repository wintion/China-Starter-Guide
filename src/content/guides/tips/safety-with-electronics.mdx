## Quick Answer
Keep devices close, avoid using them in crowded pinch points, and back up important data.

## Why this matters
Phones and wallets are essential for payments and navigation. Losing them can disrupt your trip.

## Step-by-step
1. Use a secure bag and keep devices in inner pockets.
2. Avoid using phones near doors on crowded transport.
3. Enable screen locks and find my device features.
4. Back up photos and important documents regularly.
5. Verify locally for any theft hotspots in your area.

## Common problems & fixes
- Phone slips from pocket: use a strap or zippered pocket.
- Device lost: use tracking features and contact your provider.
- Battery drains fast: reduce screen brightness and close apps.

## Checklist
- [ ] Secure bag or pocket used.
- [ ] Screen lock enabled.
- [ ] Backups completed.
- [ ] Tracking features activated.
