## Quick Answer
Choose central lodging, use direct transport, and plan for slower days with more rest.

## Why this matters
Long walks and crowded stations can be tiring. A slower pace keeps travel enjoyable.

## Step-by-step
1. Select lodging near transport hubs with elevator access.
2. Use taxis or ride hailing for the last mile.
3. Plan fewer sights per day and add rest stops.
4. Keep medical information and contacts accessible.
5. Verify locally for accessibility at attractions.

## Common problems & fixes
- Long walks in stations: use a taxi for the next leg.
- Crowds are overwhelming: visit early or choose quieter sites.
- Fatigue builds: add an extra rest day.

## Checklist
- [ ] Central lodging selected.
- [ ] Direct transport options ready.
- [ ] Rest stops scheduled.
- [ ] Medical info accessible.
