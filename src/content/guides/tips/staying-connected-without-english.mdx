## Quick Answer
Use translation apps, saved addresses, and simple phrases to communicate essential needs.

## Why this matters
You will need to communicate for transport, food, and check-in. A simple toolkit reduces stress.

## Step-by-step
1. Save your hotel address and key places in the local language.
2. Use translation apps for short phrases and show the screen.
3. Keep a small paper card with key phrases and numbers.
4. Use map pins to show where you want to go.
5. Verify locally for any service specific requirements.

## Common problems & fixes
- Translation app fails: use offline packs or a paper note.
- Staff cannot read your phone: increase text size or show a printed card.
- Miscommunication about price: ask for the total in writing.

## Checklist
- [ ] Addresses saved in local language.
- [ ] Translation app ready offline.
- [ ] Paper card with key phrases.
- [ ] Map pins prepared.
