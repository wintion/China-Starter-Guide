## Quick Answer
Ask before photographing people, be careful in sensitive locations, and follow posted rules.

## Why this matters
Respecting privacy builds trust and avoids conflicts.

## Step-by-step
1. Look for no photo signs in museums or transport areas.
2. Ask permission before photographing individuals.
3. Avoid photographing security areas, checkpoints, or staff without permission.
4. Keep camera noise and flash minimal in quiet spaces.
5. Verify locally for venue specific rules.

## Common problems & fixes
- Someone objects to a photo: apologize and delete it if asked.
- Rules are unclear: ask staff before taking photos.
- Crowded areas: keep your camera close and avoid blocking traffic.

## Checklist
- [ ] Rules checked at the venue.
- [ ] Permission requested for people shots.
- [ ] Flash disabled where appropriate.
- [ ] Respect for privacy maintained.
