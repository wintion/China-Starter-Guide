## Quick Answer
Use official transport, keep your phone charged, and plan your route before you leave.

## Why this matters
Late night travel offers fewer options and can be confusing in unfamiliar areas.

## Step-by-step
1. Confirm your route and destination before leaving.
2. Use official taxi queues or a ride hailing app.
3. Keep your phone charged and avoid isolated streets.
4. Share trip details with a friend or travel companion.
5. Verify locally for late night transport hours.

## Common problems & fixes
- Pickup point unclear: move to a well lit landmark and update the pin.
- Low battery: stop to charge before ordering a ride.
- Unofficial driver offers a ride: decline and use official transport.

## Checklist
- [ ] Route confirmed before departure.
- [ ] Phone charged and backup power ready.
- [ ] Official transport used.
- [ ] Destination address saved.
