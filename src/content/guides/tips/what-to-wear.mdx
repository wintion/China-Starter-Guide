## Quick Answer
Dress for comfort and the weather. Bring layers and choose modest options for temples or formal settings.

## Why this matters
Clothing affects comfort, mobility, and how you are perceived in public spaces.

## Step-by-step
1. Check weather forecasts for each city and pack layers.
2. Bring comfortable walking shoes for long days.
3. Pack modest options for temples or formal visits.
4. Keep a light jacket for air conditioned spaces.
5. Verify locally for any dress codes at specific sites.

## Common problems & fixes
- Too hot during the day: switch to breathable layers and carry water.
- Shoes not suitable: buy a basic pair locally if needed.
- Dress code issues: keep a scarf or light jacket for coverage.

## Checklist
- [ ] Layers packed for weather changes.
- [ ] Comfortable walking shoes ready.
- [ ] Modest option available.
- [ ] Light jacket for indoor AC.
