## Quick Answer
Use the metro if you have light luggage and the line is open. Choose Didi or an official taxi for door to door travel, especially late at night or with heavy bags.

## Why this matters
Your first trip sets the tone for the whole visit. Picking the right transfer saves time, money, and stress when you are tired from the flight.

## Step-by-step
1. Check arrival time and locate metro, taxi, and shuttle signs in the terminal.
2. Decide on metro vs taxi vs Didi based on luggage, time of day, and destination. Verify locally for operating hours.
3. For metro, buy a ticket or open your transit QR in a wallet app, then follow line signs to your station.
4. For taxis, go to the official taxi queue and show your destination in Chinese if possible.
5. For Didi, open the app, set the pickup point, confirm the license plate, and pay in app.

### Traveler scenarios
- Scenario: You land late and the metro is closed.
  Resolution: Use the official taxi queue or a Didi pickup point and confirm the meter or in app fare.
- Scenario: You have two large suitcases and a long walk to the metro.
  Resolution: Use taxi or Didi for a direct ride and avoid stairs and transfers.

## Common problems & fixes
- Unofficial drivers approach you: decline and use the official queue or in app pickup.
- Pickup point confusion: use airport signage and move to a clearly marked pickup zone.
- Payment fails at exit: use cash or a backup card and resolve the wallet later.

## Checklist
- [ ] Destination address saved in Chinese and English.
- [ ] Wallet app ready or cash available.
- [ ] Pickup point confirmed if using Didi.
- [ ] Backup method ready for late arrivals.
