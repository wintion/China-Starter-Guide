## Quick Answer
Book in an official app or buy at the station. Use your passport details and verify locally if the app flow changes.

## Why this matters
Tickets can sell out during busy periods. A clear booking process helps you secure seats early.

## Step-by-step
1. Search routes in the booking app and compare times and stations.
2. Select a train and seat class, then enter passenger details as shown on your passport.
3. Pay in app and save the booking confirmation.
4. If buying at a station, bring your passport and request the train number and seat class.
5. Check your ticket details before travel and verify locally for pickup rules.

### Traveler scenarios
- Scenario: No seats left on your preferred train.
  Resolution: Choose another departure time or consider a different city pair.
- Scenario: Your name does not match the ticket.
  Resolution: Rebook with exact passport spelling and cancel the old ticket if possible.

## Common problems & fixes
- Payment fails: use a backup card or wallet and try again.
- App shows different station names: confirm the Chinese characters before booking.
- Ticket cannot be found at the gate: open the booking app and show the confirmation.

## Checklist
- [ ] Route and station names confirmed.
- [ ] Passport details entered exactly.
- [ ] Booking confirmation saved offline.
- [ ] Backup plan ready if sold out.
