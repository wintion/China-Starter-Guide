## Quick Answer
Flights are best for longer distances or when train seats are limited. Check in early and keep your passport and booking ready.

## Why this matters
Airports can be large and busy, and baggage rules vary. Knowing the flow prevents surprises.

## Step-by-step
1. Compare flight time vs train time before booking and verify locally for schedule changes.
2. Check in online or at the airport counter and keep your boarding pass.
3. Pass security with your passport and boarding pass ready.
4. Confirm your gate and listen for boarding announcements.
5. Collect baggage and plan your airport to city transfer.

### Traveler scenarios
- Scenario: Your flight is delayed and you will miss a connection.
  Resolution: Contact the airline desk and consider switching to a later flight or train.
- Scenario: Your bag is overweight.
  Resolution: Move items to your carry on or pay the fee if acceptable.

## Common problems & fixes
- Gate changes: check the airport screens and app alerts.
- Boarding pass not accepted: visit the service desk with your passport.
- Baggage delay: file a report before leaving the baggage area.

## Checklist
- [ ] Train vs flight decision made.
- [ ] Passport and boarding pass ready.
- [ ] Baggage rules checked.
- [ ] Transfer plan for arrival.
