## Why take the train
High-speed rail is usually faster than flying for city pairs under 800 km, with downtown stations and frequent departures.

## Booking options
### Official apps
- The 12306 app is the official booking channel.
- Passport verification can take time, so register early.

### Ticket counters
- Major stations have English signs and staffed counters.
- Bring your passport; it is required for ticket pickup.

## Seat classes
Second class is the best value for most travelers. First class offers wider seats and quieter cabins.

## Boarding day checklist
### Security and gates
Arrive 45 to 60 minutes early for security and ticket checks.

### Luggage rules
Carry-on limits are generous, but oversized bags may be checked at the platform.

## Common mistakes
- Going to the wrong station in the same city.
- Confusing the carriage number with the seat number.
- Missing the gate close time.
