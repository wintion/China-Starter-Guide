## Quick Answer
High speed rail is fast and reliable for many intercity trips. Arrive early, pass security, and board by car number.

## Why this matters
Stations can be large and busy. Knowing the flow reduces stress and helps you avoid missing the train.

## Step-by-step
1. Check your train number, departure time, and station name.
2. Arrive early and follow signs to security and ticket checks. Verify locally for station rules.
3. Locate your waiting area using the gate and car number on your ticket.
4. Board when the gate opens and match your seat and car number.
5. Listen for arrival announcements and prepare to exit quickly.

### Traveler scenarios
- Scenario: You arrive at the wrong station in the same city.
  Resolution: Use a taxi or Didi to reach the correct station and recheck your ticket.
- Scenario: You cannot find your car number.
  Resolution: Ask staff or follow platform signs for car ranges.

## Common problems & fixes
- Ticket not found: confirm your booking app and passport details.
- Gate closes while you are in line: ask staff for help or rebook the next train.
- Seat confusion: compare the seat number on the ticket with the overhead sign.

## Checklist
- [ ] Station name confirmed.
- [ ] Passport and ticket ready.
- [ ] Gate and car number located.
- [ ] Luggage packed for quick exit.
