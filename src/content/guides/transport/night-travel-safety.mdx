## Quick Answer
Use official transport, stay in well lit areas, and keep your phone charged. Avoid unmarked cars.

## Why this matters
Late night travel reduces transport options. A clear plan keeps you safe and reduces stress.

## Step-by-step
1. Confirm your route before leaving and save the destination in Chinese.
2. Use official taxi queues or Didi pickup points at night.
3. Keep your phone charged and avoid isolated areas.
4. Share your trip details with a companion if possible.
5. Verify locally for station hours and night service changes.

### Traveler scenarios
- Scenario: The last metro has stopped running.
  Resolution: Use the official taxi queue or Didi and confirm the fare in app.
- Scenario: A driver asks you to cancel the ride.
  Resolution: Decline and rebook through the app for safety.

## Common problems & fixes
- Few taxis available: wait in a well lit area or move to a main entrance.
- Low phone battery: stop at a shop to recharge before ordering a ride.
- Unclear pickup point: use a landmark and message the driver.

## Checklist
- [ ] Route saved before departure.
- [ ] Phone charged and backup power ready.
- [ ] Official transport used only.
- [ ] Destination address ready in Chinese.
