## Quick Answer
Expect security screening at stations and airports. Keep your passport and ticket ready and follow staff instructions.

## Why this matters
Security lines can be long, and the flow may be unfamiliar. A prepared routine keeps you on schedule.

## Step-by-step
1. Arrive early and follow signs for security or check in.
2. Place bags on the belt and remove restricted items if requested.
3. Keep your passport and ticket accessible for inspection.
4. Retrieve your items and repack away from the belt.
5. Verify locally for any special screening rules.

### Traveler scenarios
- Scenario: Your bag is flagged for inspection.
  Resolution: Cooperate, open the bag, and follow staff instructions.
- Scenario: You forgot to bring your passport.
  Resolution: Return to retrieve it or rebook if there is no time.

## Common problems & fixes
- Long lines: arrive earlier and avoid last minute transfers.
- Confusing signage: ask staff and show your ticket.
- Prohibited item found: surrender it if required or store it before security.

## Checklist
- [ ] Passport and ticket ready.
- [ ] Liquids and electronics organized.
- [ ] Extra time added for security.
- [ ] Boarding gate confirmed after screening.
