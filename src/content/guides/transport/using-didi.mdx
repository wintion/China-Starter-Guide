## Quick Answer
Didi is a reliable door to door option. Set an accurate pickup point, confirm the license plate, and pay inside the app.

## Why this matters
Ride hailing is useful when the metro is crowded or you have luggage. A clear pickup point prevents delays and cancellations.

## Step-by-step
1. Open Didi and allow location access for accurate pickup placement.
2. Set your pickup point on the map and add a landmark if the area is busy.
3. Choose a ride type and confirm the estimated pickup and route.
4. Match the license plate and driver name before getting in.
5. After arrival, confirm the fare and pay in app.

### Traveler scenarios
- Scenario: The driver calls in Chinese and you do not understand.
  Resolution: Use the in app chat with quick replies or send a short message with your location.
- Scenario: The pickup point is blocked by traffic.
  Resolution: Move to a nearby side street and update the pickup pin.

## Common problems & fixes
- Driver cancels: rebook with a clearer pickup point and avoid busy exits.
- App shows a different route: confirm the destination and adjust before starting.
- Payment fails: switch to a backup card or wallet and try again.

## Checklist
- [ ] Pickup pin placed accurately.
- [ ] License plate confirmed before entering.
- [ ] Payment method set in app.
- [ ] Backup transport option ready.
